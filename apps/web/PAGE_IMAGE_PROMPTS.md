AplicoCV — Page Background Image Prompts (fully individualized)

Scope: full-bleed background images for the PUBLIC content pages (Blog and the other
marketing/legal pages). Excluded by request: the landing page and the authenticated app
("admin portal"). The auth login/register backgrounds already exist and are not redone.

Each prompt below is completely self-contained: the brand colors, mood, lighting, camera,
composition, realism rules and a full negative prompt are written out individually for that
single image — nothing is shared or abbreviated. Brand colors for reference, woven uniquely
into each brief: electric blue #3392ff, violet #8f6cff, cyan #1fbef0, deep navy #0b1426,
clean white.

People appear only where they reflect real product use (job seekers applying, preparing,
getting hired). Where a single human subject is featured she is a beautiful young woman in
her twenties, depicted professionally and tastefully; ethnicity is varied across pages.
Pages B1–B5 are intentionally person-free.
